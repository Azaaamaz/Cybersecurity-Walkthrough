# What is Firewall and how its work?

## 1. Overview

Firewall is a device responsible to determine what traffic is allowed and not allowed to enter network.

## 2. Key Content

Firewall = Device responsible to determining traffic that enter network

Factors that come into consideration is:

- Where is traffic coming from?
- Where is the traffic going to?
- What port is the traffic for?
- What protocol is the etraffic using?

Firewall came in different shape and size, from hardware to software. It could be categorized into 5 categories, below are 2 of them

- Statefull
==> Use intire information from a traffic to determining the traffic, using more resorce but smarter

- Stateless
==> only checking individual packet, dumber and only work effectively only if there is a rule to define what could enter or not. Not requiring much of resource
