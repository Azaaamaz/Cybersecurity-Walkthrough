# What is Packet and Frame?

## 1. Overview

Packet is a piece of data, meanwhile frame is used at layer 2 (Data Link) to add additional info, so the packet could be sent. Your data is not sent a whole.

## 2. Key Concept

Packet = Piece a data that will be sent to a device, data is divided into several pieces

Frame = Additional information to sent data into the target
